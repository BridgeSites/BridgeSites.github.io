What we build

Powerful Features Everything you need for seamless device integration

Smart Notifications

Get all your iPhone notifications instantly on your Wear OS watch with rich previews and quick actions.

Notification History

Access your notification history from your iPhone. Browse past notifications anytime, anywhere.

Health & Fitness

Sync workouts, heart rate, sleep data, and activities with complete iPhone Health app integration. Full Apple Health compatibility.

Media Controls

Control music playback, adjust volume, and manage podcasts directly from your watch.

Contact Sync

Keep your contacts synchronized between your iPhone and Wear OS watch for seamless communication.

Privacy First

End-to-end encryption and secure data handling. All data processed locally — never stored on external servers.

Camera Remote

Use your Wear OS watch as a remote camera shutter for your iPhone. Perfect for photos and videos.

Seamless Sync

Automatic background syncing of health data, notifications, and settings between your devices.

File Manager

Manage photos, videos, files, and apps on your watch. Browse, upload, download, and organize content from your iPhone.

Compatibility

Compatible Devices Works with Wear OS 4.0+ watches (except Oppo and OnePlus)

● Supported

Wear OS 4.0+

All watches running Wear OS 4.0 or later are fully supported for all features.

Samsung Galaxy Watch Google Pixel Watch Fossil Gen 6 & more
● Not Supported

Oppo & OnePlus Watches

Due to manufacturer customizations, Oppo and OnePlus watches are currently not compatible with Bridge.

Oppo Watch OnePlus Watch
● Easy Check
